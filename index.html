This Maze Generator is based on the youtube videos Here

In the videos he takes a grid and removes walls between cells to carve out a maze using depth first search and backtracking algorithms.

Below, the "Small, Bigger, Biggest" drop-down determines how big the maze is while the "Slow, Medium, Fast" buttons determine the speed at which the maze is carved out. Try selecting "Slow" then "Small" to see what's actually happening with the algorithm! Click the "No Grid" button to see the maze carved out without the grid shown!
