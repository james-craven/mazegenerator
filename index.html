This Maze Generator is based on the youtube videos Here

In the videos he takes a grid and removes walls between cells to carve out a maze using depth first search and backtracking algorithms.

I, however, inversed this process. I start with a blank square and draw walls instead of remove walls to carve out the maze.

On the left, the "Easy, Medium, Hard" buttons determine how big the maze is while the "Slow, Faster, Fastest" buttons determine the speed at which the maze is carved out. Try clicking "Slow" then "Easy" to see what's actually happening with the algorithm!
